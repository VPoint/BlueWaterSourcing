About Us

Our Story

Complete Classroom Canada was established in 2002. We are a product-supply company. We supply materials and products to numerous companies around the world. Our expertise enables us to cater to you demands in a timely and cost efficient manner.

After a change in management, we revitalized the companies organization to better serve our customer’s needs.

Our Mandate

Our goal as an organization is to provide products and services to our customers at the highest quality, on time and for the lowest cost.

Our promis is that we will do this in the most environmentally sustainable and reliable process.

Director's Profile

Omohlegoho Raji
Director

The owner and director of Blue Water Sourcing Inc., Ms Omoh Raji graduated with a Masters degree in Education and a Post-Graduate diploma in Early Childhood Education from York University, Toronto Ontario Canada. As an Early Childhood Educator, she designed and implemented developmental appropriate activities that included opportunities for language development as well as hands-on exploration of the school environment. She has been teaching and was responsible for the creative writing and program management for young children for over fifteen years now. She is the author of “The Turtle’s Back” a children story book. She also provided a variety of learning experiences designed to meet children’s needs, utilized multicultural sensitive approaches and resources in the classroom setting. Happily married, blessed with children and presently manages Blue Water Sourcing Inc in Ottawa Ontario, Canada where she resides with her family.
